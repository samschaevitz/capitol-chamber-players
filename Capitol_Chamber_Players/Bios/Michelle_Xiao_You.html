Michelle Xiao You

 

MICHELLE XIAO YOU, violin, a multiple competition winner, Ms. You has been featured as a soloist with some of the finest symphonies in the world, including the Houston Symphony, the BBC Northern Symphony in Great Britain, the Central Philharmonic of China (now the National Orchestra of China), and many other fine orchestras, including the Sacramento Philharmonic, the Camellia Symphony, the Merced Symphony and the Auburn Symphony. Ms. You has also given recitals in the United States, Europe, and China.

Ms. You, a native of Shanghai, China, began her studies with her father and continued with professors Situ H.C. and Z.L. Wang at the Central Conservatory of Music in Beijing. After coming to the United States, she furthered her studies with Dorothy DeLay and Kurt Sassmanshaus at the University of Cincinnati. She then moved to Sacramento and continued her career as a concert violinist and teacher.
