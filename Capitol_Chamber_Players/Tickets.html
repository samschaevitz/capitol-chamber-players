Tickets

 
 


Season pass rates for concerts are listed to the left.


Individual tickets are available at the door at $18 for general admission, $12 for seniors, and $7 for students. These tickets may be used one-at-a-time for each of our four concerts, for example, or four people may use them at one concert. Include a self-addressed and stamped envelope with your check. Otherwise, tickets will be held at the door. Your canceled check is your receipt. Make check payable and return this order form to:

       

    Capitol Chamber Players

    Davis, CA 95617
